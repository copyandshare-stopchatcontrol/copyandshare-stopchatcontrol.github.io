Hello, I'm sharing this message to raise awareness about a cause that matters to me.

In a few days, a law will be voted on, known under several names:
COM(2022) 209
CSAM Regulation (Child Sexual Abuse Material Regulation)
Chat Control 2.0 (used by opponents)

The EU wants to scan all our messages, even encrypted ones, weaken encryption, and introduce mass surveillance for 450M Europeans.

In a few days (on 14 October 2025), if the vote is favorable, this law will be
definitively adopted.

As I write this, the petitions with 50,000 and 30,000 signatures are insignificant compared to 450 million Europeans or 68 million French people, and far too few seem aware when I ask at random.

If, like me, you are against this draft law,
I invite you to share this message with as many people as possible
to help move the situation in the right direction.

I prefer to act everywhere at once, and that's what I'm doing.
However, WhatsApp would largely be enough if everyone shared this message with all their contacts.
